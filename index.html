Fabric Technology

Engineered confidence, woven into every stitch.

What is GSM?

GSM means grams per square meter. It measures fabric density and richness. Higher GSM indicates stronger structure and better drape.

Why 220 GSM is Premium

220 GSM offers superior fall, long life, and polished finish without feeling heavy. It's ideal for elevated basics.

Built for Indian Climate

Our knits and weaves are breathable, moisture-aware, and designed to remain comfortable across warm and humid environments.

Pre-shrunk & Colorfast

Pre-shrunk finishing keeps fit stable. Colorfast dyes retain depth and reduce fading even after repeated washes.

Skin-safe Materials

Bio-washed fabrics and controlled finishing reduce irritation for daily wear comfort across long workdays.
