Fabric Technology

Why NIRAA feels premium from the first wear.

What is GSM?

GSM (grams per square meter) measures fabric density and body. It directly impacts drape, durability, and tactile richness.

Why 220 GSM is Premium

220 GSM cotton creates a structured yet soft silhouette that maintains form and elevates everyday basics.

Built for Indian Weather

Breathable construction and moisture-aware yarn behavior ensure comfort in warm and humid climates.

Pre-Shrunk Precision

Pre-shrunk processing protects garment dimensions and keeps fit consistency after multiple washes.

Colorfast Dyes

High-grade dyes retain tone depth and resist fade, preserving a premium visual finish.

Skin-Safe Finishing

Bio-washing and controlled finishing reduce roughness and improve comfort for long daily wear.
